The Feed is the first ever coded website done by myself, Kala Yamaguchi.
It features a fun outgoing theme that mimics the edge and playfullness
of youth culture. As an artist, I wanted this website to express a lot of myself
away from a professional setting. Growing into a young adult, a website
of this much freedom may be harder to attain. I hope to find some joy hopefully
one day looking back on this work of art.
