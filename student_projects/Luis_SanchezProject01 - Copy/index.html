An Analysis of Subject-22-O

About General Activity Favorites

Through this website we hope to gain more insight into the life and customs of Luis Sanchez. A Peruvian male of 19 years, he keeps to himself both in person and online, but he spends - a possibly unhealthy - amount of time scouring the vast virtual world known as the internet.

Growing up a relatively introverted and reserved child led him to take up more isolated and personal hobbies so as to keep himself occupied. For instance, he very much enjoys playing and even watching videogames, it is something that can maintain his interest for a long time and help keep him active mentally, but unfortunately not physically.

Surprisingly, he has quite the affinity for art and has spent 16 of his 19 years trying his damndest to get better at the craft. He really wishes he had more time to draw, but alas, university and general lack of creativity have left his instagram account barren and his mind frustrated with its inability to express itself. How sad. He really does love art however, it has even manifested into a form of obsession with any kind of artistic medium - mainly anime and manga. He has been quite influenced by these aforementioned art forms, and he hopes he can instill this same kind of influence and inspiration unto others when he is more capable.

Since he has never cared much for social practice or interaction, he has a minimal social media presence, but he very much enjoys using these outlets in his spare time - which he sure has a lot of. As a result, one will usually catch him scrolling through memes on Instagram - which have deteriorated his sense of humor to comical levels - or reading random tidbits of news and useless facts on Reddit.

If you are so lucky to catch him outside of these two timesinks, fret not, he is still very much distracted and wasting his time. You will certainly catch him watching or listening to countless hours of music, videos and streams through the holy procrastination trinity that is Spotify, Youtube, and Twitch.

I am sure this eloquent summary we have drafted for Luis has painted him to be a very diligent and approachable person, yes?

Upon analysis and instruction by our research subject, it seems we may have focused a bit too much on some...less favorable traits, and he wishes to end it on a more personal note. We can assure you Luis definitely does not just procrastinate all day. Absolutely not. He really enjoys what he does in university and is very interested by the prospects that Computer Science and Design can open together. Furthermore, he has become quite a bit less introverted with age - thankfully - and he's glad he gets to experience new and exciting things with new people. To close, we hope you enjoy delving into the wonderously...simple life of Luis, he takes a lot of pride in keeping it as such.
